How do I buy from Cnfans
Finding the items
Step 1
Method 1
You can check to find the goods you like or interested in Taobao www.taobao.com, Tmall www.tianmao.com , 1688, www.1688.com, Weishop www.weidian.com, these platforms;
Method 2
You can create Reddit, Discord, Instagram, Tiktok, Youtube and other social or video media to create an account, by searching Cnfans, Follow some communities or people, and look through their spreadsheets to find links to your favorite products.
Using product links
Step 2
Once you've decided on a link to a product you'd like to buy, all you have to do is copy and paste the link into the search box on the Cnfans website. Alternatively, you can use the links in the form to find the products you want to buy directly. Once you have found the item you want to buy on Cnfans, you will need to select the details of the item you want to buy, such as the color, size, version, etc. If you have any special needs, be sure to leave a note. If you have any special needs, be sure to leave a note stating so.
Deciphering versions and batches: projects may have different versions or batches, each with their own quality and functionality. To determine the best version of the project you want, visit platforms such as Yupoo, where you can find cataloged images and detailed comparisons. These comparisons will help you make an informed decision about the version you want to choose.
View QC item photos
Step 3
To check actual photos of products instead of stock images, you can use the qc.photos website. Simply paste a link to the original product to access a comprehensive photo gallery. This feature allows you to scrutinize items from all angles and even view additional photos paid for by the user.
Calculate the cost
Step 4
Cnfans offers a helpful tool called the Estimator, which is accessed by adding items to your cart and clicking on it. This tool provides information about weight, dimensions and shipping costs. By selecting the appropriate shipping line and adjusting options, such as removing the shoebox to reduce the quantity, you can accurately estimate the total cost of your purchase.
Submit your order
Step 5
Once you have finalized your selections and viewed the cost, go ahead and add the items to your shopping cart. During the checkout process, fill in the necessary details, agree to the terms and conditions, and complete payment. If PayPal is not available, you can top up your balance and pay with the balance amount.
Order Processing and shipping
Step 6
After placing an order, it will go through several stages. Once an agent is appointed and the item is purchased from the seller, the status will be changed from "Pending" to "Purchased". Subsequently, when the seller sends the item to the warehouse, it will change to "Seller Shipped". At this point, you will receive a courier tracking section to monitor the progress of the shipment within China. Eventually, when the warehouse confirms the arrival of your item, the status will change to "Warehouse Received".
Quality check and shipping options
Step 7
Once your item arrives at the warehouse, it will enter the quality check phase. You will receive QC photos for inspection. After verifying the condition of the item, you can proceed with the shipment. Shipping options will be displayed, allowing you to select the option that best suits your needs. Keep in mind that faster shipping options may incur higher costs.
Complete shipping
Step 8
When you are satisfied with the QC photos and shipping options, confirm the shipment. Your item(s) will be prepared, packaged and shipped to the designated shipping address. You will receive a tracking number that will allow you to monitor the progress of your package until it arrives at your doorstep.
By following this comprehensive guide, you will be fully prepared for the process of purchasing items from Cnfans. Remember to join the relevant community, take advantage of the tools available, and make informed decisions when choosing products and shipping methods. Happy shopping!
